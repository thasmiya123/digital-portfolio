SEO Audit: The Bagel Bar

🔍 Title Tag Analysis

Current Title Tag: The Bagel Bar Coffee House | Food, Beverage & Catering

Issue: No location or keywords included

Suggestion: Bagel Café in Dubai | The Bagel Bar Coffee House & Catering

🔤 H1 Tag Analysis

Current H1: The Bagel Bar Coffee House | Food, Beverage & Catering

Issue: Missing location keywords and SEO phrases

Suggestion: Fresh Bagels, Coffee & Catering in Dubai

📸 Image Optimization

Issue: Images on the homepage are missing alt tags, which are important for SEO and accessibility.

Suggestion: Add descriptive alt text such as alt="Fresh blueberry bagel with cream cheese"

🚀 Page Speed

Tool Used: Google PageSpeed Insights

Issue: Mobile score below 40; large images and unused JavaScript are slowing down the load time.

Suggestion: Compress images using TinyPNG, defer unused scripts, and enable browser caching.

📱 Mobile Usability

Issue: Buttons and navigation are not fully mobile-responsive. Text is hard to read without zooming.

Suggestion: Improve CSS layout with mobile-first design and larger, spaced buttons.

📍 Local SEO

Issue: No mention of the location “Dubai” in website content. No Google My Business listing found.

Suggestion: Add "Dubai" in footer, contact page, and headings. Claim Google Business Profile and request reviews.

🔑 Keyword Research

  • Primary Keywords: bagel café Dubai, fresh bagels Dubai, coffee shop in Dubai
  • Secondary Keywords: breakfast places in Dubai, best bagels near me, café with WiFi in Dubai
  • Search Intent: Commercial and local — customers searching for places to eat nearby

🛠️ Recommendations Summary

  • Add keyword-rich meta title and description
  • Use proper H1 and H2 heading structure
  • Compress and optimize images with alt tags
  • Improve mobile responsiveness
  • Add local keywords like “Dubai” throughout content
  • Claim and optimize Google My Business profile
  • Create blogs like “Top 5 Bagel Cafés in Dubai” targeting local search terms
